OEM Process

OEM Bag Manufacturing Process

Learn the typical OEM custom bag process from inquiry, material confirmation and sample development to bulk production and shipment.

Ask For AdviceAll Guides

In This Guide

Inquiry and requirement confirmationQuotation and sample developmentBulk production and quality controlPacking and shipment

Inquiry and requirement confirmation

The process starts with product type, size, material, logo, quantity, color, packing and reference design. A clear brief helps the factory recommend the right solution.

Quotation and sample development

After confirming the specification, the supplier prepares pricing and sample details. Sample development confirms material, construction, logo and packaging before bulk order.

Bulk production and quality control

Bulk production usually includes material preparation, cutting, logo process, sewing, trimming, inspection and packing. Quality checks should happen before shipment.

Packing and shipment

Export packing can include polybag, hang tag, barcode label, inner box and shipping carton. Buyers should confirm carton mark and delivery method before shipment.
