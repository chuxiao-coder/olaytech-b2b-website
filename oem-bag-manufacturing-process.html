01

Inquiry And Specification Confirmation

The OEM process starts with a clear product brief. The more complete the information, the more accurate the supplier's quotation and sample plan will be.

  • Prepare bag category, size, use scenario and reference photos.
  • Confirm material, color, logo method and accessory requirements.
  • Share target quantity, packing method and delivery timeline.
  • Mention target market if the product is for retail, promotion or private label.
02

Quotation And Sample Planning

After reviewing the requirement, the supplier evaluates feasibility, cost structure and sample development details.

  • Quotation should match the confirmed material and construction.
  • Sample cost and sample lead time should be clear.
  • Artwork and logo placement should be reviewed before sample production.
  • Potential risk points should be discussed early.
03

Sample Review And Approval

Sample approval is the key control point before bulk production. It confirms not only appearance but also structure, workmanship and buyer expectation.

  • Check size, material, color and hand feel.
  • Review stitching, zipper, lining, logo position and packing.
  • Request adjustments before bulk production begins.
  • Keep the approved sample as the inspection reference.
04

Bulk Production, QC And Shipment

After sample approval, the production process should follow the confirmed standard. Quality control and packing checks help protect the final delivery.

  • Production follows approved material, sample and logo details.
  • QC checks measurements, workmanship, function, logo and packing.
  • Carton marks, quantity and shipping details should be confirmed.
  • Final communication should remain clear until shipment is completed.
