01

Inquiry And Requirement Confirmation

The OEM process starts with a clear brief. The factory needs enough information to confirm feasibility and quote properly.

  • Product category and target use.
  • Material, size, color and logo method.
  • Quantity, packaging and delivery schedule.
02

Quotation And Sample Development

After reviewing the requirement, the supplier confirms cost direction, sample details and possible risk points.

  • Sample confirms structure and workmanship.
  • Artwork placement should be checked before bulk order.
  • Material and color should match buyer expectations.
03

Bulk Production And Quality Control

After sample approval, production follows the confirmed standard and quality control supports consistency.

  • Cutting, sewing and branding follow the approved sample.
  • QC checks size, stitching, material and logo appearance.
  • Packing details should be confirmed before shipment.
04

Packing And Shipment

Final export preparation includes individual packing, carton arrangement and delivery coordination.

  • Confirm polybag, carton marks and quantity.
  • Review shipment schedule and delivery needs.
  • Keep communication clear until goods are dispatched.
