Buyer Questions

Useful answers before a new custom bag order.

What custom bags can O'Lay manufacture?

We support OEM/ODM projects for cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers, card storage products and other custom soft bags.

Can you help if we only have a reference sample?

Yes. A photo, sketch, reference sample or rough specification is enough to begin discussion. We can help check material, structure, logo method, size and packing options.

Do you support logo customization?

Yes. Depending on the bag material and brand style, projects may use printing, embroidery, woven labels, rubber patches, zipper pullers or other practical branding methods.

What information should buyers send for a quote?

Please share product type, size, material, logo requirements, quantity, packing method and target delivery market. Clear details make quotation and sample planning faster.
