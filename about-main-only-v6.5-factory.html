Buyer Questions

Useful Details Before You Start

These short answers help buyers understand how O'Lay supports custom bag projects.

What types of custom bags do you manufacture?

We produce cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers and other OEM/ODM soft bags.

How fast can you develop samples?

For many stock fabrics, sample development can usually take 3–7 days. Custom materials or special structures may need more time.

Do you support OEM and ODM projects?

Yes. We support custom size, material, color, structure, logo application, packing and bulk manufacturing requirements.
