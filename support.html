OEM / ODM Process

From Idea To Bulk Production

Use this process as a checklist before starting a new custom bag order.

01

Send Inquiry

Share product type, quantity, material, size, logo and reference photos.

02

Confirm Details

We review structure, material, logo method, packing and target use.

03

Sample Development

A sample can be made to confirm shape, hand feel, logo and function.

04

Bulk Production

Cutting, logo process, sewing, trimming and production follow the approved sample.
