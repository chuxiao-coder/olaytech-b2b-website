Logo Guide

Logo Methods For Custom Bags

Understand printing, embroidery, woven labels, rubber patches, metal plates and debossed logos for custom bags.

Ask For AdviceAll Guides

In This Guide

Screen printing and heat transferEmbroidery and woven labelsRubber patch, leather patch and metal plateChoose by material and budget

Screen printing and heat transfer

Printing is suitable for flat panels, simple graphics and promotional orders. Heat transfer can support more colorful artwork and works well for polyester, nylon and some synthetic fabrics.

Embroidery and woven labels

Embroidery gives a premium textured look and is common for cosmetic bags, canvas pouches and lifestyle products. Woven labels are flexible and work across many materials.

Rubber patch, leather patch and metal plate

Patches and plates are good options for mid-to-high-end bags. They help create stronger brand recognition on cooler bags, travel organizers, sports bags and cosmetic cases.

Choose by material and budget

Logo method depends on fabric, surface texture, artwork detail, order quantity and brand positioning. If you are unsure, send your logo file and reference product photos for advice.
