Canvas And Cotton

Natural look, strong texture and suitable for tote bags, cosmetic pouches, gift bags and retail packaging.

Nylon And Polyester

Lightweight, flexible and suitable for sports bags, travel bags, drawstring bags and organizers.

Oxford Fabric

Durable and cost-effective for outdoor bags, cooler bags, tool bags and travel accessories.

PVC / EVA / TPU

Clear, waterproof or structured options for cosmetic bags, toiletry bags and travel wash pouches.

Neoprene

Soft, protective and flexible for lunch cooler bags, bottle sleeves, makeup pouches and tech organizers.

RPET And Eco Materials

Suitable for buyers who need recycled material stories, promotional programs or sustainability positioning.
