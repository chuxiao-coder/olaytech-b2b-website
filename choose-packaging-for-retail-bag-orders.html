Packaging Guide

How To Choose Packaging For Retail Bag Orders

Choose polybag, hang tag, barcode label, carton packing and retail presentation for custom bag orders.

Ask For AdviceAll Guides

In This Guide

Basic export packingRetail packagingCarton and shipping marksMatch packaging to sales channel

Basic export packing

Most wholesale bag orders use individual polybags and export cartons. This option is efficient for bulk shipment and B2B distribution.

Retail packaging

Retail programs may require hang tags, barcode labels, brand stickers, insert cards or custom printed packaging. These details should be confirmed before quotation.

Carton and shipping marks

For importers and retailers, carton size, carton weight, carton mark and barcode requirements may be important for warehouse receiving and logistics.

Match packaging to sales channel

Promotional orders can use simple packaging, while retail and gift programs often need stronger presentation. Share your sales channel so the supplier can recommend suitable packing.
