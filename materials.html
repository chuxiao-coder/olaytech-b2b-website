Material Selection

Choose Materials Based On Use Case And Brand Positioning

For B2B buyers, material choice affects product cost, hand feel, durability, print method and final retail presentation.

  • Canvas and cotton for natural reusable bags
  • Nylon and polyester for lightweight sports and travel use
  • PVC, EVA and TPU for clear waterproof pouches
  • RPET and non woven options for promotional programs
