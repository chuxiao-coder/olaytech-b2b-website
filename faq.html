FAQ

Custom Bag Manufacturing FAQ

Answers for B2B buyers about OEM bags, logo customization, samples, quotation details and export support.

What products does Olaytech manufacture?

We manufacture cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers, card binder cases and OEM custom bags.

Can I customize logo and packaging?

Yes. Logo methods include printing, embroidery, woven labels, rubber patches, metal plates and zipper pulls. Packaging can be customized for retail or bulk shipment.

What information should I send for quotation?

Please send bag type, size, material, quantity, logo requirement, packaging need and reference images.

Do you support sample development?

Yes. We can review your design and help confirm material, structure, logo and sample details before bulk production.

Which markets are suitable for your bags?

Our bags are suitable for beauty, travel, retail, food storage, outdoor, sports, promotional gifts and private label programs.

How fast can you reply?

Our sales team usually replies by email or WhatsApp within 24 hours after receiving complete product details.
