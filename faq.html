Frequently Asked Questions

Custom Bag Manufacturing FAQ

Common questions about OEM bag production, sampling, MOQ, logo customization, materials and quotation details.

What products can Olaytech manufacture?

We support cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers, card binder cases and custom OEM bag projects.

Can you make custom logo bags?

Yes. We support printing, embroidery, woven labels, rubber patches, metal plates, zipper pulls and retail packaging.

What information is needed for quotation?

Please provide product type, size, material, quantity, logo method, packaging requirement and reference images.

Can you help with material selection?

Yes. We can suggest canvas, nylon, Oxford, PVC, EVA, TPU, neoprene, felt, RPET, PU leather and other materials based on use case and budget.

Do you support sample development?

Yes. Sample development can be arranged after confirming product structure, size, material, logo method and target quantity.
