What custom bags can Olaytech manufacture?

We manufacture cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers, card binder cases and custom OEM bags.

Can I add my logo?

Yes. We support printing, embroidery, woven label, rubber patch, leather patch and other logo methods depending on material and design.

Can you make samples before bulk order?

Yes. Send your reference image, size, material and logo requirement. We can help confirm sample direction before bulk production.

Where should I upload product images?

Upload product photos to the assets/products folder using the file names shown in each image slot, such as cosmetic-bags-01.jpg.

How do I request a quotation?

Use the Contact page and share product type, size, material, quantity, logo and reference images. We will reply by email or WhatsApp.
