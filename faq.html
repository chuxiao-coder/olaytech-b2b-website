FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions For Custom Bag Orders

Useful answers for B2B buyers who want custom logo bags, wholesale bags or OEM ODM product development.

Can you make custom logo bags?

Yes. We support logo printing, embroidery, woven labels, rubber patches, metal logos, embossing and customized packaging based on the material and product structure.

What information should I provide for a quotation?

Please provide product type, size, material, color, logo method, quantity, packaging requirement, target market and reference images if available.

Do you support OEM and ODM projects?

Yes. We can help with material selection, structure development, sample making, bulk production and export packaging for custom bag projects.

Which product categories do you manufacture?

We manufacture cosmetic bags, toiletry bags, shopping bags, cooler bags, sports bags, drawstring bags, travel organizers, care binder cases and other custom OEM bags.

Can you help develop products for European and American markets?

Yes. We can recommend materials, styles, logo methods and packaging directions suitable for beauty, travel, retail, outdoor, sports and promotional buyers.
