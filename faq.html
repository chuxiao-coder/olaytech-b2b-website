FAQ

Custom Bag Manufacturing FAQ

Answers for buyers preparing custom bag projects.

What information should I send for quotation?

Product type, material, size, quantity, logo method, packing request and destination country.

Can you make samples before bulk production?

Yes. We can support sample development before confirming bulk production details.

What logo methods are available?

Printing, embroidery, woven label, rubber patch, metal plate and other customized options.

Can I upload my own product photos later?

Yes. Replace the image files with the same filenames in the assets folder to update the catalog.
