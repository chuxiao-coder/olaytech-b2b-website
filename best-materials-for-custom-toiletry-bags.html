Material Guide

Best Materials For Custom Toiletry Bags

Compare nylon, PVC, EVA, TPU, canvas and waterproof linings for custom toiletry bags and travel wash bags.

Ask For AdviceAll Guides

In This Guide

Nylon and polyesterPVC, EVA and TPUCanvas and cottonChoose material by market position

Nylon and polyester

Nylon and polyester are lightweight, cost-effective and suitable for travel toiletry bags, hanging wash bags and organizer pouches. They work well with lining, mesh pocket and zipper structures.

PVC, EVA and TPU

Clear PVC, EVA and TPU are suitable for waterproof or transparent toiletry bags. They are often used for travel sets, wash pouches and retail packaging programs.

Canvas and cotton

Canvas and cotton give a natural look and are suitable for promotional toiletry pouches, gift sets and lifestyle brands. They can support printing, embroidery and woven labels.

Choose material by market position

For budget promotional programs, polyester and PVC are common. For premium travel or beauty brands, nylon, TPU, canvas and structured EVA options can create a higher perceived value.
